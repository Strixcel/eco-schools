WHAT IS ECO-SCHOOLS?

Eco-schools is an international program that helps schools become more environmentally friendly by encouraging students, teachers, local authorities and community to work together in making the school a greener place

Objective

Improve the ecological footprint and the sustainability performance of the school

Aim

Eco-schools is an international initiative designed to help make every school sustainable and to bring about behaviour change in students, staff and their families

Our Project Ideas

  • Tiffin it! (Zero Plastic)
    • An initiative to reduce the use of single-use plastics and promote the adoption of sustainable alternatives in the school compound
  • The Peat (Compost Fertilizer)
    • Compost fertilizer is the result of the decay of organic materials, its content is high with nutrients that can stimulate plant growth. Regular use of compost reduces the need for chemical fertilizers
  • Olien-Nation (Reused Cooking Oil
    • A drive-thru will be built at the waste house in Seafield for parents to drop off oil at specific times.
  • The Reservoir (Water Filtration
    • Rainwater harvesting & filtering. The process of rainwater harvesting involves collection and storage of rainwater with the help of artificially designed systems
